Common questions about polarized sunglasses in Ahmedabad

How are polarized sunglasses better than normal sunglasses?

Polarized lenses contain a special filter that blocks horizontal glare bouncing off roads, water and glass. This gives sharper, more comfortable vision than ordinary tinted sunglasses, especially while driving in Ahmedabad.

Do your polarized sunglasses also have UV protection?

Yes. Every polarized pair at Shree Hari Chasma Ghar offers full UV400 protection that blocks harmful UVA and UVB rays along with glare, keeping your eyes safe in strong Gujarat sunlight.

Can I get polarized sunglasses made in my eye power?

Yes. After a free eye test we fit prescription polarized lenses into your chosen frame, including single vision and progressive options for clear, glare-free vision.

How do I check if my sunglasses are truly polarized?

We demonstrate it in store using a polarization test card and a screen check, so you can confirm the glare-cutting effect before you buy.
