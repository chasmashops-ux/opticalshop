Common questions about anti glare glasses in Ahmedabad

What does anti glare coating do for my glasses?

Anti glare, or anti-reflective, coating removes distracting reflections from the lens surface so screens, lights and headlights look clearer. It reduces eye strain and helps your eyes feel less tired after long work hours.

Are anti glare glasses good for night driving in Ahmedabad?

Yes. Anti-reflective lenses cut the halos and scatter from oncoming headlights and street lights, giving you sharper, more comfortable vision on Ahmedabad roads after dark.

Can I add anti glare coating to my existing power glasses?

The coating is bonded during lens making, so we fit fresh anti-glare lenses into your current frame. After a free eye test we match your exact prescription and have most pairs ready the same day.

Do anti glare glasses also block blue light from screens?

We stock anti-glare lenses that also include a blue-cut layer, ideal for long computer and mobile use. Tell us your daily screen time and we will recommend the right combination.
