Which types of contact lenses do you sell in Ahmedabad?

Do I need an eye test before buying contact lenses?

Yes. We carry out an eye test and measure your cornea so the contact lens power, base curve and diameter fit correctly and feel comfortable all day.

Can I get coloured contact lenses for daily wear?

Yes. We keep coloured and zero-power cosmetic contact lenses in natural and bold shades, available in plain and prescription power for everyday wear.

Will you teach me how to wear and care for contact lenses?

Of course. Our team demonstrates safe insertion, removal and cleaning, and recommends the right lens solution and replacement schedule for healthy eyes.
