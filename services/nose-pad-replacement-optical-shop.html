Common questions about nose pad replacement in Ahmedabad

How much does nose pad replacement cost in Ahmedabad?

Nose pad replacement is an affordable, budget-friendly repair at our New Ranip shop. The exact charge depends on the pad type, such as soft silicone, push-in or metal screw-in, and is confirmed before we begin the work.

Do I need to leave my glasses for nose pad replacement?

No. Most nose pad replacements are done while you wait in just a few minutes. We change the pads, adjust the arms and let you try the glasses on before you leave the store.

Can you replace nose pads on rimless and metal frames?

Yes. We replace screw-in nose pads on rimless and metal frames and push-in silicone pads on acetate frames. Bring any glasses with worn, yellowed, loose or missing pads and we will refit them.

Will new nose pads stop my glasses from slipping?

Yes. Fresh soft silicone nose pads grip better and we realign the pad arms to your nose, which stops glasses sliding down and reduces the red pressure marks worn pads often leave behind.
