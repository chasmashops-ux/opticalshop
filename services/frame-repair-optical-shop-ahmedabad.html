FAQs

Common questions about frame repair in Ahmedabad

Can you repair a broken spectacle hinge in Ahmedabad?

Yes. We repair and replace broken hinges, tighten loose screws and re-align temple arms on metal and plastic frames, with most hinge jobs finished the same day at our New Ranip store.

Do you fix snapped or cracked frames?

We solder and weld snapped metal frames and re-join cracked plastic fronts where the frame allows. If a frame is beyond safe repair, we transfer your existing lenses into a new frame instead.

How long does frame repair take and what does it cost?

Simple jobs like screw tightening, nose-pad swaps and adjustments are often done in minutes. Soldering and part replacements usually finish the same day. We share an honest price before starting any repair.

Can you replace nose pads and adjust frames that hurt?

Yes. We replace worn or yellowed nose pads, fit silicone grips and re-adjust frame fit so your glasses sit comfortably without slipping or pressing on your nose and ears.
