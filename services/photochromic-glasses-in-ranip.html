Common questions about photochromic glasses in Ranip

What are photochromic glasses and how do they work?

Photochromic glasses use light-adaptive lenses that automatically darken in bright sunlight and turn clear again indoors. The UV in sunlight triggers the colour change, so one pair works as both clear eyeglasses and sunglasses.

Can I get photochromic lenses with my eye power in Ranip?

Yes. At our Ranip store we fit photochromic lenses with single vision, bifocal or progressive power after a free computerized eye test, so you get clear adaptive vision tailored to your prescription.

How long do photochromic lenses take to change colour?

Quality photochromic lenses darken within seconds in direct sunlight and fade back to clear in about one to two minutes when you move indoors, giving smooth all-day comfort.

Do you offer blue cut photochromic glasses for screen use?

Yes. We stock photochromic lenses with a blue cut coating that filter harmful blue light from phones and computers indoors while still darkening outdoors, ideal for mixed daily use in Ahmedabad.
