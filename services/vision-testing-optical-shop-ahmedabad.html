Do you provide computerized vision testing in Ahmedabad?

Yes. Our Ahmedabad optical shop uses a computerized auto-refractor along with a trial-frame check by our optometrist to measure your exact eye power accurately.

How long does a vision test take at your store?

A complete vision test usually takes 15 to 20 minutes. You get a clear prescription on the spot and can choose frames and lenses straight away.

Is the eye testing free if I buy glasses?

Yes. When you order eyeglasses or sunglasses with us, your vision testing and lens fitting are done free of charge at our New Ranip store.

Can I get glasses made the same day after a vision test?

For most standard powers we offer same-day fitting. High-power, progressive or special-coated lenses may take a little longer depending on the prescription.
