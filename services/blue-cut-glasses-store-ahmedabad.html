FAQs

Common questions about blue cut glasses in Ahmedabad

What are blue cut glasses and how do they work?

They use a special lens coating that filters part of the high-energy blue light from screens, helping reduce glare, strain, and visual discomfort.

Who should use blue cut glasses?

Students, office professionals, gamers, designers, and anyone spending several hours per day on digital devices can benefit from them.

Do blue cut glasses require an eye test first?

Yes. We recommend a free computerized eye test so the blue cut lens can be paired with the right power if you need vision correction.

Can I get blue cut glasses without power just for screen protection?

Yes. We offer zero-power blue cut options for people with normal vision who want digital comfort without prescription lenses.
