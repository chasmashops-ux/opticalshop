What are blue cut glasses and how do they help screen users in Ahmedabad?

Blue cut glasses use a special lens coating that filters part of the high-energy blue light from phones, laptops and TV screens. This reduces glare and digital eye strain, helping screen users in Ahmedabad stay comfortable through long study or work hours.

Can I get zero-power blue cut glasses if my vision is normal?

Yes. We offer zero-power blue cut glasses for people with normal vision who simply want stronger screen comfort and glare reduction, with no prescription required.

Do you do a free eye test before fitting blue cut lenses?

Do blue cut glasses also have anti-glare coating?

Yes. Our blue cut lenses come with an anti-glare coating that cuts reflections from screens, indoor lighting and night driving, giving clearer and more relaxed vision.
