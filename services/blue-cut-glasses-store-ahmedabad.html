Frequently Asked Questions

What are blue cut glasses and how do they work?

Blue cut glasses use a special lens coating that filters out a portion of high-energy blue light emitted by phone, laptop, and TV screens, helping reduce digital eye strain and improving visual comfort during long screen sessions.

Who should use blue cut glasses?

Students attending online classes, office professionals working on computers, gamers, and anyone spending more than 3-4 hours a day on digital screens can benefit from blue cut lenses.

Do blue cut glasses require an eye test first?

Yes, we recommend a free computerized eye test before fitting blue cut lenses, so the coating is paired with the correct power if you also need vision correction.

Can I get blue cut glasses without power, just for screen protection?

Yes, we offer zero-power blue cut glasses for people with normal vision who only want protection from screen glare and blue light.

How is blue cut different from anti-glare coating?

Anti-glare coating reduces surface reflections and glare on the lens, while blue cut coating specifically filters blue light wavelengths from screens. Many of our lenses combine both for all-round screen comfort.
