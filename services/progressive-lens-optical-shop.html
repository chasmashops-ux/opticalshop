FAQs

Common questions about progressive lenses in Ahmedabad

What are progressive lenses and who needs them?

Progressive lenses combine distance, intermediate and near vision in one lens with no visible line. They suit anyone over 40 dealing with presbyopia who wants clear vision at every distance from a single pair of glasses.

Are progressive lenses better than bifocals?

Yes for most wearers. Progressive lenses give a smooth, line-free transition between distances, add a clear intermediate zone for screens and dashboards, and look like normal single-vision glasses, unlike lined bifocals.

How long does it take to adjust to progressive lenses?

Most people adapt within a few days to two weeks. Accurate measurements and correct frame fitting, which we do in store, make adaptation faster and reduce any side-to-side blur.

How much do progressive lenses cost in Ahmedabad?

Progressive lens prices depend on the design grade, lens material and coatings. At our New Ranip shop we stock options from affordable everyday progressives to premium wide-field designs, with honest pricing after a free eye test.
