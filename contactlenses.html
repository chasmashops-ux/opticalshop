FAQs

Contact lens questions, answered

Which contact lenses are best for first-time users?

Daily disposable lenses are best for beginners — they are hygienic, need no cleaning, and you wear a fresh pair every day. We fit them after a quick eye test.

Do you provide color contact lenses in Ahmedabad?

Yes. We stock branded color and natural-look lenses in zero power and with power, in both daily and monthly options.

Can I get contact lenses for astigmatism?

Yes. We provide toric contact lenses designed for astigmatism, fitted accurately after a computerized eye test.

Is an eye test required before buying contact lenses?

Yes. A contact lens eye test and fitting helps choose the right power, base curve and lens type for safe, comfortable wear. Our eye testing is free.
